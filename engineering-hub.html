Our Methodology

Engineering Process

A proven step-by-step approach ensuring project success

01

Project Initiation

Define scope, objectives, and requirements. Conduct site visits and data collection.

02

Conceptual Design

Develop preliminary designs, trade-off studies, and cost estimates (±30-40% accuracy).

03

Basic Engineering

Detailed design development, equipment specifications, and cost refinement (±15-20%).

04

Detailed Engineering

Final design, construction drawings, procurement packages, and accurate costing (±5-10%).

05

Construction Support

Site supervision, quality control, change management, and progress monitoring.

06

Commissioning & Handover

System testing, performance verification, training, and project closeout.
