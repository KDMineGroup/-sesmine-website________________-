Key Metrics

Essential Economic Indicators

NPV (Net Present Value)

Present value of all future cash flows minus initial investment. Positive NPV indicates value creation.

IRR (Internal Rate of Return)

Discount rate at which NPV equals zero. Higher IRR indicates better investment opportunity.

Payback Period

Time required to recover initial investment. Shorter payback indicates lower risk.

Breakeven Point

Production level where total revenue equals total costs. Critical for operational planning.
